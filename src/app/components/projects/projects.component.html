Al Reesh Foodstuff LLC – Corporate Website (Ongoing)

🛒 Al Reesh Foodstuff LLC is a Dubai-based company specializing in high-quality food product distribution. This project involves building a professional, scalable, and user-friendly website representing the company’s brand and serving customers, partners, and job seekers.

🔹 Key Features

  • ✅ Home Page: Clean and modern design with company highlights.
  • ✅ About Us: Showcasing company history, mission, and values.
  • ✅ Product Catalog: Organized categories with search and filter options.
  • ✅ Brands Showcase: Highlighting trusted partner brands.
  • ✅ Contact Page: Contact form, Google Maps, WhatsApp/email links.

🚀 Upcoming Features

  • Career Portal: Job listings with online application system.
  • Admin Dashboard: Secure admin access to manage content.
  • Multilingual Support: English + Arabic.
  • SEO Optimization: Metadata, sitemap, and performance improvements.
  • Blog / News Section: Company news and industry insights.
  • Client Login Portal (Future): Bulk order pricing and inquiry tracking.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: Angular (dynamic UI/UX)
Backend: Node.js + Express.js
Database: MongoDB
Hosting/Deployment: Vercel / AWS (planned)
Other Tools: JWT Authentication, RESTful APIs, Responsive Design (mobile-first)

🌟 Outcome (Expected)

A modern digital presence reflecting Al Reesh Foodstuff’s brand identity.
Improved accessibility for customers, partners, and job seekers.
Scalable structure growing from corporate site to feature-rich client platform.
